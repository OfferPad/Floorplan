## Palettes

### Orange

OfferPad has an official orange color to represent the brand, "pumpkin" (`#ff6c2c`). Additionally, a richer, higher-contrast orange, "tangelo" (`#fa4b00`), is used for text emphasis and other uses.

### Blue
